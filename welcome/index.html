The Wild Tomato has been selected as a finalist for USA Today’s 10 Best Gluten Free Pizzerias in America!  We are honored to have our tasty food and dedicated staff recognized in a very competitive market.

We promise every one of our Gluten-Free, Vegan and Dairy-Free customers:

  • Gluten Free isn’t just another menu item.
  • All our Gluten Free Products are stored separately from other items.
  • Gluten-Free Products are handled with specific utensils and trays.
  • We only use Corn Flour in the kitchen to eliminate contamination.
  • We will gladly discuss your allergies and concerns regarding any item you order.
  • We will continue to expand our Gluten Free menu as we find products and recipes up to your standards.
